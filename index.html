learner

Identifying the Target User

  • Tech literate and has internet access through some device
  • Enjoys consuming video content in genres/on topics they find interesting
  • Has sufficient competence to understand and enjoy foreign language media

Motivations

  • Finding authentic materials of the appropriate level poses a significant barrier
  • Even in classroom settings, learners aren't all of the same level so assigned materials will be boring for some and too difficult for others
  • Watching foreign shows and videos is often more 'fun' than reading texts found in foreign language textbooks

theory

Principles and Practice in Second Language Acquisition

Krashen (1982)

  • Input Hypothesis - learners acquire competence by noticing that the target L2 form (syntactic or lexical) is just beyond their competence and harnessing ‘extra-linguistic information’ to comprehend these structures
  • Affective Filter Hypothesis - motivation, self-confidence and anxiety (among other motivational factors) can significantly impact the efficacy of comprehensible input

The Optimal Input Hypothesis: Not All Comprehensible Input is of Equal Value.

Krashen (2020)

  • comprehensible - at the right level
  • rich - language should contribute to the meaning
  • abundant - many instances of a structure
  • compelling - an engaged learner will extract the most benefit from comprehensible input (and is likely to devote more time to compelling materials)

Revelations from Three Consecutive Studies on Extensive Reading

Lee (2007)

  • One semester
  • Taiwanese University students
  • Group A. assigned English readings and
  • Group B. could choose readings from a catalogue
  • A outperformed B on CLOZE testing
  • A also reviewed their media more favorably

A personalized recommendation-based mobile learning approach to improving the reading performance of EFL students.

Hsu, Hwang, & Chang, (2013)

  • Taiwanese senior high school EFL students
  • Mobile tech recommended texts to students based on performance
  • Groups with recommender outperformed the control group significantly by end of term
  • AI-driven recommendation algorithms?
  • Recommend other media?

activity

Given our range of learners and these theoretical underpinnings, the aim is to create an engaging and low-stakes activity which promotes ease of access to level-appropriate materials. To further its accesibility, the proposed CALL technology should be available on both mobile and desktop. A wide array of content is needed in order to give sense of freedom and ensure a range of people can find something to suit their interests.
